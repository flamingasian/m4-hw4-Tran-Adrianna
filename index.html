About

Paramore is an American rock band from Franklin, Tennessee formed in 2004. The current members consists of lead vocalist Haley Williams, guitarist TaylorYork, and drummer Zac Farro. Williams and Farro are founding members of the group, while York joined in 2007. The band is signed to Fueled by Ramen, a subsidiary of Atlantic Records, both owned by Warner Music Group.
